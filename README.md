1️⃣ Project Title/Headline

Super Store Sales Performance Dashboard

2Purpose / Short Description
This dashboard is designed to analyze and monitor the overall sales performance of the Super Store. It enables users to explore key business metrics including total sales, quantity sold, profit levels, and delivery timelines. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by revealing trends, regional performance differences, customer behavior insights, and product segment contributions.This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Super Store’s sales performance across different regions, product categories, and time periods. It helps decision-makers track key business metrics, analyze sales trends, and identify revenue-driving segments.

3️⃣ Tech Stack

Power BI — Data modeling, visuals, and interactivity

4️⃣ Data Source

Superstore Sales Dataset (Sample retail dataset including orders, shipments, product information, and customer details)

5️⃣ Features / Highlights
Displays core KPIs: Total Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Avg. Delivery Days
Filter by Region to analyze performance in Central, East, South, and West
Sales Trend Analysis by month and year (2019 & 2020)
Sales by Segment & Category for product performance insights
State-wise Map Visualization to track sales distribution geographically
Payment Mode & Ship Mode Analysis to understand customer purchasing preferences
Sub-category Breakdown highlighting top revenue contributors
User-friendly and visually engaging interactive dashboard design
